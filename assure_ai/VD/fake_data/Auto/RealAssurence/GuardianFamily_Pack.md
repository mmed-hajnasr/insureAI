# RealAssurance: Guardian-Family Safety Pack
**Product ID:** FA-AUTO-GUARD-2026
**Category:** Multi-Driver & Family Protection
**Status:** Synthetic Data Model

## 1. Executive Summary: The Guardian-Family Philosophy
The **Guardian-Family Pack** is designed for households with multiple drivers, prioritizing safety, peace of mind, and logistical simplicity. While other packs focus on the vehicle or the environment, this pack focuses on the *people* inside. It is the only pack that offers "Floating Liability," where coverage follows the family members rather than just the primary vehicle.

---

## 2. Comprehensive Inclusions (The "Safety" List)
The Guardian-Family pack includes specific protections that address the risks of shared family vehicles and young drivers.

### 2.1. Multi-Driver & Youth Protection
* **Student-Away Coverage:** If a dependent (under 25) is away at university, they remain covered when driving any third-party vehicle (with owner permission) up to a limit of $50,000.
* **First-Accident Forgiveness:** The first "at-fault" accident involving a driver under 21 will not result in a premium surcharge, provided the driver completes a mandatory RealAssurance Safety Module.
* **Child Safety Seat Replacement:** Following any collision (regardless of severity), RealAssurance provides an immediate $500 voucher per seat for the replacement of all child safety seats installed in the vehicle.

### 2.2. Family Logistics & Wellness
* **School-Run Continuity:** In the event of a breakdown during designated school drop-off/pick-up hours, RealAssurance dispatches a vetted "Secure-Transport" vehicle to complete the school run for the children.
* **Post-Traumatic Counseling:** Up to 10 sessions of professional trauma counseling for any family member present in the vehicle during a severe collision.
* **Pet Injury Coverage:** Coverage up to $1,500 for veterinary expenses if a household pet is injured while traveling in the insured vehicle.

### 2.3. Damage & Convenience
* **Interior "Life-Proof" Protection:** Coverage for accidental damage to the interior, including significant spills, upholstery tears, or screen damage to rear-seat entertainment systems.
* **Key & Remote Replacement:** Coverage for lost or stolen "Smart Keys" and remotes, including the cost of reprogramming the vehicle's ignition system.

## 3. Strict Exclusions (The "Boundary" List)
To maintain affordable rates for families, certain high-risk scenarios are excluded:

### 3.1. Commercial & Professional Exclusions
* **Unregistered Ride-Sharing:** Any incident occurring while the vehicle is being used for commercial ride-sharing (e.g., Uber/Lyft) unless a "Commercial Rider" was explicitly purchased.
* **Courier & Delivery:** Damage sustained during the delivery of goods, food, or parcels for profit.

### 3.2. Usage & Maintenance Exclusions
* **Long-Term Storage Neglect:** Damage caused by rodents or battery leakage if the vehicle is left stationary and uninsured-for-storage for more than 90 consecutive days.
* **Non-Household Drivers:** Regular use (more than 3 times per week) by an individual not residing at the registered policy address who is not listed as a secondary driver.

## 4. The Unique "Family-Trust" Mechanism
The Guardian-Family pack operates on the **Trust-Circle Verification System (TCVS)**.

1.  **Shared Safety Score:** The family’s premium is based on a weighted average of all drivers. High scores from experienced drivers can "offset" the higher risk profile of younger drivers.
2.  **Geofencing Alerts:** Policyholders can set "Safe Zones" via the RealAssurance app. If a secondary driver exits a Safe Zone (e.g., traveling out of state), the policy remains active but triggers a real-time notification to the primary policyholder.
3.  **The "Safety Swap":** Every 2 years of claim-free driving allows the family to swap their deductible for a "Premium Freeze," ensuring their rate stays the same even if national insurance taxes rise.

## 5. Legalities and Regulatory Framework
Subject to the *Real Family Protection Act (2023)*.

* **Definition of Household:** For the purposes of this policy, "Household" includes all blood relatives, spouses, and legal dependents residing at the same physical address.
* **Privacy Controls:** While the TCVS monitors safety, RealAssurance legally commits to never sharing location-specific "Safe Zone" logs with third-party advertisers or law enforcement without a judicial warrant.
* **Duty of Disclosure:** Any change in a dependent's driving license status (e.g., suspension) must be reported within 48 hours or the "First-Accident Forgiveness" clause is voided.
