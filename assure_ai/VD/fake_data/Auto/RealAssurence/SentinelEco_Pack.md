## 2. Comprehensive Inclusions (The "Green" List)
The Sentinel-Eco pack includes high-value protections tailored for modern vehicle technologies and city living.

### 2.1. Electric & Hybrid Specifics
* **Battery Health Guarantee:** Coverage against sudden battery cell failure or degradation exceeding 30% of original capacity within the first 5 years (not caused by improper charging).
* **Charging Infrastructure Protection:** Liability and damage coverage for home charging stations (Wallboxes) against surges, vandalism, or theft.
* **Public Charging Liability:** Protection if your vehicle or charging cable causes a trip-and-fall hazard or electrical damage to third-party public infrastructure.

### 2.2. Urban Mobility & Micro-Transit
* **Multi-Modal Transit Credit:** If the insured vehicle is in the shop for more than 24 hours, RealAssurance provides $50/day in credits for e-scooter, bike-share, or public transit apps instead of a traditional rental.
* **Smart-City Collision:** Specialized coverage for collisions with non-traditional road users, including delivery robots, autonomous shuttles, and e-bikes.
* **Congestion Zone Waiver:** Reimbursement of up to 5 "Ulez" or Congestion Charge fines per year if the driver can prove an emergency detour was required due to a covered accident.

### 2.3. Digital & Software Assets
* **Over-the-Air (OTA) Update Protection:** Coverage for "Bricking" incidents where a manufacturer-pushed software update renders the vehicle inoperable.
* **Privacy Data Breach:** A $1,000 allowance for identity restoration services if your vehicle's telematics data is leaked via a third-party manufacturer server breach.

## 3. Strict Exclusions (The "Non-Eco" List)
To keep premiums low for sustainable drivers, the following are strictly excluded:

### 3.1. High-Emission & High-Risk Activities
* **Combustion Engine Swaps:** Any modification involving the installation of an internal combustion engine in an EV or Hybrid.
* **Off-Roading:** Damage sustained on unpaved roads, trails, or construction sites (unless the vehicle is specifically rated for such terrain and the driver was on a mapped road).
* **Trailer/Towing Overload:** Damage caused by towing loads exceeding the vehicle's official EV-rated towing capacity (common in urban EVs).

### 3.2. Technical Exclusions
* **Third-Party Firmware:** Use of non-OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) software "jailbreaks" to unlock battery range or acceleration.
* **Grid-to-Vehicle (G2V) Misuse:** Damage caused by using the vehicle as a home battery backup system if not explicitly supported by the manufacturer's warranty.
