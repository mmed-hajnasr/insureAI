# Yahaj: Zenith Fleet Executive Pack
**Agency:** Yahaj Assurance
**Policy Code:** YH-AUTO-ZENITH-2026
**Target:** Corporate Executives and Luxury Fleet Owners

## 1. Pack Philosophy
The **Zenith Fleet Pack** is a high-concierge, zero-friction insurance product. It treats the vehicle not as a machine, but as a business tool that must never be unavailable. It focuses on time-preservation and prestige.

---

## 2. Inclusions (The "Executive" List)
* **Zero-Downtime Guarantee:** In the event of an accident or breakdown, a replacement luxury sedan is delivered to the driver's GPS location within 60 minutes.
* **Chauffeur Liability:** Coverage for professional drivers and bodyguards, including professional indemnity for the chauffeur's actions.
* **Prestige Repair Network:** All repairs are performed exclusively by OEM master technicians using 100% original parts, with a lifetime guarantee on paint matching.
* **Executive Security Detail:** In high-risk urban areas, if a breakdown occurs at night, Yahaj dispatches a private security escort to stay with the driver until the vehicle is recovered.

---

## 3. Exclusions (The "Conduct" List)
* **Track Days:** Any high-speed use on private circuits.
* **Leasing/Sub-letting:** Coverage is void if the vehicle is rented out to third parties or used for non-executive commercial purposes.
* **Driver Identification:** Coverage is only active for pre-cleared "Executive Drivers" who have passed the Yahaj Background Check.

---

## 4. How the "Zenith-Concierge" Mechanism Works
The pack includes a **Biometric Ignition Integration**. The policy premium is adjusted based on the verified identity of the driver. If a non-authorized driver starts the vehicle, coverage reverts to "Passive Theft" mode only.

---

## 5. Legalities
* **Confidentiality Clause:** Yahaj guarantees that all dashcam footage and GPS data are stored on encrypted servers and are exempt from standard data-sharing agreements with third-party marketing firms.
* **Arbitration:** Disputes are handled by the *International Chamber of Commerce (ICC)* to ensure corporate-level adjudication.
