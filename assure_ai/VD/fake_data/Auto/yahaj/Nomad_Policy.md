# Yahaj: Nomad Adventure Pack
**Agency:** Yahaj Assurance
**Policy Code:** YH-AUTO-NOMAD-2026
**Target:** Off-road, Overlanding, and Cross-border Travelers

## 1. Pack Philosophy
The **Nomad Pack** is built for those who treat their vehicle as a gateway to the unknown. It is the only policy that ignores traditional "paved road" constraints, offering full indemnity in remote locations and across international borders within the MENA and Mediterranean regions.

---

## 2. Inclusions (The "Expedition" List)
* **Off-Road Recovery:** Specialized winch-and-tow services from unmapped areas, sand dunes, or remote mountainous terrain up to 200km from the nearest asphalt.
* **Cross-Border Continuity:** Automatic activation of local third-party liability coverage when crossing into recognized neighboring territories (Green Card equivalent included).
* **Equipment Floater:** Up to $10,000 coverage for non-permanent overland gear, including roof-top tents, portable fridges, and satellite communicators.
* **Interim Vehicle Repatriation:** If the vehicle is immobilized abroad, Yahaj pays for the flatbed return of the vehicle to the home country while providing airfare for the passengers.
* **Natural Force Indemnity:** Full coverage for sandstorm damage, flash floods, and wildlife encounters.

---

## 3. Exclusions (The "Safety" List)
* **Political Volatility:** Damage sustained during active war, civil insurrection, or in "No-Go Zones" as defined by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
* **Competition/Rallying:** Participation in the Dakar Rally or any amateur desert racing events.
* **Mechanical Neglect in Extreme Heat:** Failure to maintain cooling systems specifically rated for 45°C+ environments.

---

## 4. How the "Nomad-Sync" Mechanism Works
Yahaj utilizes **Satellite Telemetry (Sat-Sync)**. Even in areas with zero cellular coverage, the Nomad Pack remains active via a low-orbit satellite handshake. This allows for SOS triggering and location-based risk assessment in real-time.

---

## 5. Legalities
* **Jurisdictional Flexibility:** Claims can be filed and adjudicated in any Yahaj-partnered territory.
* **Environmental Responsibility:** The insured is liable for environmental cleanup if a fuel leak occurs in protected national parks.
